Au Pair Program Preparation

Preparing responsible, caring and culturally aware young adults for international Au Pair opportunities and cultural exchange experiences.

Carefully Screened Candidates

Each applicant undergoes an initial screening process including identity verification, background review, childcare motivation assessment and communication evaluation.

Childcare & Family Readiness

Candidates are guided on childcare responsibilities, cultural adaptation, family integration, household expectations and professional conduct before being introduced to host families.

Communication Skills

We encourage candidates to develop strong communication skills, language confidence and interpersonal abilities that support successful family placements.

Cultural Exchange Focus

Our approach promotes cultural exchange, personal growth and mutual respect between host families and Au Pair participants.

Our Commitment To Host Families

TOPELA GLOBAL understands the importance of trust when welcoming an Au Pair into your home.

For this reason, we encourage all applicants to demonstrate maturity, responsibility, honesty, adaptability and a genuine interest in supporting family life and childcare activities.

Candidates are expected to respect the laws, culture, values and household rules of the host country while maintaining a positive and professional attitude throughout their stay.

Our goal is to help connect host families with motivated individuals who are eager to learn, contribute positively and participate in a meaningful cultural exchange experience.

About TOPELA GLOBAL

International recruitment, workforce mobility and travel support.

TOPELA GLOBAL is connected with TOPELA TRAVELS, a registered company operated by Michael Akinsola. The company focuses on travel guidance, documentation support, candidate coordination and verified international opportunities.

For anyone who would like to read further about me, my name is Michael Akinsola. I am originally from Nigeria and currently based in the Netherlands, where I live, work and build professional international partnerships.

Before moving into business and international networking, I served in the Nigerian Army for 12 years. That experience helped shape my discipline, leadership, organization, communication skills and ability to work with people from different backgrounds.

Having lived in Europe for over two years, with exposure to the Netherlands, Spain, Germany, France and Ireland, I understand both European employer expectations and the challenges many qualified Nigerians face when looking for genuine legal work opportunities abroad.

My goal is to build a transparent, professional and long-term recruitment structure that benefits employers, recruitment agencies and reliable workers.
